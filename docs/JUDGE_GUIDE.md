## 🔍 Key Features to Notice

When reviewing each page, keep an eye out for these advanced operational capabilities:

### 1. INDEX Score Hero & Telemetry Count
* Renders real-time leading indicators.
* Note the live counts and telemetries drifting in the hero section, simulating continuous physical telemetry ingestion.

### 2. Causal Priority Drivers & Causal Graph (PRIME)
* View the **Top System Drivers** card list mapping the most pressing macroeconomic risks.
* Click one of the "Example Causal Presets" (e.g., *Red Sea disruption*) to watch the Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) animate its propagation path down to final retail inflation, backed by audited AI agent consensus.

### 3. Explainability Ledger (FORECAST & SITUATION ROOM)
* Look for the **"WHY ARTHAM BELIEVES THIS"** cards. This layout maps raw signals directly to expected outcomes, grounding predictions in empirical reality.

### 4. Strategic Threat Risk Monitor (INDEX & SITUATION ROOM)
* Renders real-time threat levels for Supply Chain, Inflation, Energy, and Trade risks that adapt dynamically to simulated shocks.

### 5. Confidence Decomposition (SITUATION ROOM & FORECAST)
* Click **[View Breakdown]** on recommended actions or forecast cards to inspect sub-confidence levels (Data Quality, Signal Agreement, Forecast Stability).

### 6. Chronos Time Machine (REPLAY)
* Select a historical crisis (e.g., *2021 Suez Canal Blockage*), click play, and observe how ARTHAM processes past event timelines.
* Notice the calibration metrics and the active `SYSTEM LEARNING` telemetry indicator.
