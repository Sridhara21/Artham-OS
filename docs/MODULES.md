# ARTHAM OS: Module Documentation & Tab Reference

Each module in the ARTHAM OS sovereign terminal serves a specific role within the economic intelligence lifecycle.

---

## 1. INDEX: Command Center HUD
*   **Purpose**: Renders the executive-level dashboard displaying the composite sovereign economic health scoreboard.
*   **Inputs**: Live feed alerts, composite shifted scores, live stock index pricing tick feeds.
*   **Outputs**: 
    *   *ARTHAM Score*: Comprehensive leading indicator index.
    *   *Strategic Risk Monitor*: Risk badges indicating Supply Chain, Inflation, Energy, and Trade threats.
    *   *Copilot Status*: Pulsing status representing current AI agent cycle state (Listening, Reasoning, Forecasting).
*   **User Value**: Offers immediate, 10-second situational awareness of the physical economy for policy makers and heads of state.

---

## 2. TWIN: National Physical Digital Twin
*   **Purpose**: Visualizes logistics flow velocities and terminal yard densities across a multi-scale spatial digital twin overlay.
*   **Inputs**: Port manifests, railroad sensor logs, highway FASTag speed registries.
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Corridor Telemetry*: Segment speeds and corridor capacity utilization rates.
    *   *Port yard latency*: Dwell times and yard container congestion.
    *   *Mandi nodes*: Anomaly price tracking overlay for agricultural hubs.
*   **User Value**: pinpoints exactly *where* structural physical blockages and delays are occurring.

---

## 3. PRIME: Causal Reasoning Engine
*   **Purpose**: Translates unstructured events or shocks into a structured Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) explaining economic transmissions.
*   **Inputs**: Natural language economic queries or preset scenario triggers.
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Causal DAG Graph*: Step-by-step visual chain linking the root cause to final inflation indices.
    *   *Agent Audit Log*: Personas evidence statements, audit trails, and confidence metrics.
    *   *Top System Drivers*: Live prioritized list of real-time macroeconomic shocks.
*   **User Value**: Explains the *why* behind complex inflationary vectors, grounding predictions in empirical data sources.

---

## 4. FORECAST: Projections Horizon
*   **Purpose**: Renders machine learning forecasts projecting logistics capacities and inflation transmission curves.
*   **Inputs**: ClickHouse historical time-series datasets, seasonal agricultural yields, shipping rates.
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Macro Forecast charts*: Leading indicators (GDP, CPI projections).
    *   *Explainability Ledger*: Tabular card mapping primary and secondary signals to predicted sectors and confidence intervals.
*   **User Value**: Predicts *what happens next* if current bottlenecks continue unmitigated.

---

## 5. SCENARIO LAB: Sovereign Stress Simulator
*   **Purpose**: A sovereign simulation sandbox allowing policy planners to stress-test customized shocks.
*   **Inputs**: Six customizable parameter sliders (Oil shock, Port disruption, Monsoon delay, Rail strike, Corridor flood, Coal shortage).
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Three-tier Area charts*: Best, Expected, and Worst-Case index projections.
    *   *Simulation Warning Log*: Expected index drop offsets warning notifications.
*   **User Value**: Enables planners to stress-test the economy under compounded facility and sovereign shocks.

---

## 6. SITUATION ROOM: Decision Intelligence Cockpit
*   **Purpose**: Recommends active logistical bypass blueprints and priority wagon allocations.
*   **Inputs**: Simulation parameters, active corridor load limits, cargo priority categories.
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Automated Action Briefs*: Step-by-step mitigation plans.
    *   *Reasoning Pathways*: Causal chains mapping the recommended plan to final GDP offsets.
    *   *Mitigation Metrics*: Cost impact, carbon footprint savings, transit hours saved, and estimated GDP leakage prevented.
*   **User Value**: Offers decision makers automated mitigation options to resolve bottlenecks.

---

## 7. REPLAY: Historical Calibration Machine
*   **Purpose**: Rewinds, pauses, and re-runs past disruptions to benchmark prediction calibration and policy performance.
*   **Inputs**: Historical event registers (e.g., 2021 Suez Canal grounding).
*   **Outputs**:
    *   *Replay Event Timeline*: Step-by-step historical event logs.
    *   *Calibration Metrics*: System accuracy logs and calibration error percentages.
*   **User Value**: Enables retrospective policy audits and optimizes neural prediction weights over time.
